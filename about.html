Our Story

The Problem We Saw

We noticed that ordering food in bulk was complicated, time-consuming, and often unreliable. Whether planning a celebration, meal prepping for the week, or feeding a crowd, people struggled to find trusted vendors who could deliver quality food in practical quantities.

Our Solution

FoodXL was born from the need to make bulk food ordering effortless. We've created a multi-vendor platform that connects you to trusted food providers, offering everything from soups and stews to rice dishes and beverages—all available in practical liter-based quantities.

Our Mission

To make bulk food discovery, delivery, and celebration effortless for everyone—from dinner parties and special events to family meals and business needs.

Our Values

Environmental Responsibility

We're committed to reducing packaging waste and carbon footprint through bulk ordering. Our platform supports sustainable practices and helps minimize food waste through efficient meal planning.

Vendor Partnership

We empower local vendors by connecting them with bulk buyers, providing seamless order management, and helping them grow their business with increased traffic and pricing control.

Quality & Trust

Every vendor on our platform is carefully vetted to ensure consistent quality, reliability, and exceptional service for every order, big or small.
